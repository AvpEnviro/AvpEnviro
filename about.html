Our story

How Avp came to life

We specialize in delivering integrated technical, security, and housekeeping services tailored to meet the dynamic needs of modern businesses. Our trained security personnel ensure round-the-clock protection for your people, property, and assets, while our expert housekeeping teams uphold the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene. Complementing these services, our technical workforce offers skilled support in facility operations, equipment maintenance, and infrastructure management. Together, our multidisciplinary teams create a safe, clean, and efficiently maintained environment—enabling your organization to operate smoothly and grow confidently.

At Avp Enviro Facilities, we are committed to providing reliable and tailored Security and Housekeeping Services that enhance safety, cleanliness, and dayto-day operational efficiency. Guided by principles of integrity, professionalism, and dependability, we serve as a trusted partner to organizations across sectors looking for smooth and effective facility management solutions. Our workforce includes well-trained personnel equipped with modern tools to meet evolving security demands and hygiene standards. From alert security guards to diligent housekeeping staff, we offer consistent, high-quality services shaped around your needs. With strong infrastructure and a client-first approach, we focus on building long-term partnerships rooted in trust, accountability, and results.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Here’s everything you need to know about working with us

What types of manpower services do you provide?

We provide trained and experienced personnel for security, housekeeping, facility management, technical services (electricians, plumbers, AC technicians), gardening, and more.

Do you offer services for both residential and commercial properties?

Absolutely. We cater to a wide range of clients, including housing societies, offices, factories, malls, hospitals, educational institutions, and more.

Can I customize the manpower as per my specific requirements?

Yes. We offer fully customizable staffing solutions based on your property size, industry type, and operational needs.

How do you ensure quality and discipline among staff?

We conduct regular site inspections, performance audits, and offer on-ground supervision. Our team also handles replacements and retraining when required.

How quickly can you deploy staff at my location?

Depending on the category and number of personnel required, we can often deploy within 24–72 hours after confirmation.

What are your service charges or pricing model?

Our pricing depends on the category of manpower, number of staff, and contract duration. Please contact us for a customized quotation.
